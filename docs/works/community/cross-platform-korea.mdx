Cross-platform Korea Meetup is an event designed to bring together developers, designers, and enthusiasts who are interested in cross-platform app development in Korea. The meetup aims to foster a community of like-minded individuals, providing opportunities for networking, collaboration, and knowledge-sharing. The event typically features expert speakers, presentations, and workshops, with topics ranging from cross-platform development tools and frameworks to best practices and case studies. Attendees can expect to learn about various technologies such as Flutter, React Native, MAUI, and other emerging tools, as well as discuss the challenges and opportunities in the cross-platform app development landscape.

If you are interested please visit https://crossplatformkorea.com
